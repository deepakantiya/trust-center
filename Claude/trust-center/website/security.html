Report a vulnerability

We welcome security research. Report findings to us safely and we'll respond fast.

How to report

Email security@example.com. For sensitive reports, encrypt with our PGP key: 0xDEADBEEFCAFEBABE (placeholder — replace with your real fingerprint).

Scope

  • *.example.com
  • Our official mobile applications
  • Our public APIs documented at https://api.example.com/docs

Out of scope

  • Social engineering of employees, vendors, or customers
  • Physical attacks against offices or data centers
  • Denial-of-service / volumetric tests
  • Findings from automated tools without working exploit demonstration
  • Self-XSS, missing security headers without exploitable impact, missing CSP without bypass

Safe harbor

We will not pursue legal action against good-faith research conducted within scope and in accordance with this policy. Avoid privacy violations, service degradation, and access to data beyond what is needed to demonstrate the issue.

Response targets

  • Acknowledgment: 2 business days
  • Triage decision: 5 business days
  • Fix timelines per severity (Critical: 7d / High: 30d / Medium: 90d)

Recognition

We maintain a public Hall of Fame for researchers (with consent). Monetary rewards may apply for in-scope, novel findings.
