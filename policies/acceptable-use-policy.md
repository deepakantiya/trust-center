## 1. Scope
All workforce members using Company systems, networks, devices, or accounts.

## 2. General Principles
- Use Company resources for legitimate business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted if it does not interfere with work, consume material resources, or violate this policy.
- You have **no expectation of privacy** on Company-owned systems; activity may be logged and reviewed.
- Comply with all laws and Company policies.

## 3. Prohibited Activities
- Sharing accounts or passwords.
- Bypassing security controls (e.g., disabling MDM, using unapproved VPNs to circumvent monitoring).
- Installing unapproved software on managed endpoints.
- Storing customer data on personal devices, personal cloud storage, or unapproved AI tools.
- Accessing data outside your job duties.
- Harassment, discrimination, illegal content.
- Cryptocurrency mining on Company resources.
- Unapproved use of generative AI with confidential or restricted data — see [AI Use Standard](../docs/ai-use-standard.md).

## 4. Endpoint Security
- Devices must be enrolled in MDM with: full-disk encryption, screen lock ≤ 5 min, EDR agent, OS patches within SLA.
- Lost / stolen devices reported to IT immediately and at minimum within 24 hours.

## 5. Email & Communications
- Treat external email as untrusted; verify unusual requests via second channel.
- Report phishing via the designated button or `phishing@[company].com`.
- Do not use personal email for Company business.

## 6. Working Remotely
- Use only trusted networks; mobile hotspot or VPN on public Wi-Fi.
- Maintain visual privacy in public spaces.

## 7. Reporting
Suspected violations: report to manager, HR, or `security@[company].com`. Anonymous reporting available via [reporting hotline].
