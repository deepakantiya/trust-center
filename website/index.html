How we protect your data

Identity & Access

  • SSO and phishing-resistant MFA for all employees
  • Least-privilege RBAC with quarterly access reviews
  • 24-hour offboarding SLA

Encryption

  • AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit
  • Centralized key management with rotation
  • Customer-managed keys for enterprise tier

Application Security

  • SAST, SCA, and secret scanning in CI
  • Annual third-party penetration test
  • Public responsible-disclosure program

Operations & Monitoring

  • Centralized logging with 24/7 alerting
  • Multi-AZ infrastructure with tested failover
  • Documented incident response with 72-hour notification

Workforce

  • Background checks for all employees
  • Security training at hire and annually
  • Quarterly phishing simulations

Vendor Management

  • Risk-tiered due diligence
  • SOC 2 / ISO 27001 review of critical vendors
  • Public subprocessor list

Privacy

We collect only the data we need to deliver our service. We don't sell personal data, ever. Customers can exercise GDPR / CCPA rights through our Data Subject Request portal.
