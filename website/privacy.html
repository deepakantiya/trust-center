Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data. Control ID: CO-06.

1. Introduction

[Company Name] ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over it. It applies to all services, websites, and products operated by [Company Name].

This policy satisfies the requirements of GDPR (EU) 2016/679, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA / CPRA), and is mapped to control CO-06 in our compliance programme.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services
  • Authenticate users and prevent unauthorised access
  • Process payments and fulfill contractual obligations
  • Respond to support requests and communications
  • Meet legal and regulatory requirements
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above or as required by law. Account data is deleted within 30 days of account closure. Audit logs are retained for 12 months. Backups are purged on a rolling 90-day schedule.

5. Data Sharing and Subprocessors

We share data only with trusted subprocessors who are contractually bound to process it securely and only on our instructions. A full list is available on our Subprocessors page.

We may disclose data when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, and will notify affected users where legally permitted.

6. International Transfers

Where personal data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and maintain supplementary technical measures (encryption in transit and at rest) to ensure an adequate level of protection.

8. Security

We protect personal data with industry-standard controls: encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256), role-based access control, multi-factor authentication for all privileged accounts, and continuous security monitoring. Our full security programme is described on the Security page.

9. Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies (session management, CSRF protection) and, with your consent, analytics cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time through your browser settings or our cookie preference centre.
