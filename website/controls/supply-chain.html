Supply Chain & Vendor Controls

Supply chain controls govern how we vet, contract, monitor, and offboard third parties who access or process Company or customer data. Our current subprocessor list is published at subprocessors.html. Evidence is collected in evidence/ vendor assessment artifacts.

Control ID Control Name Implementation SOC 2 ISO 27001 CMMC L1 CMMC L2 ISO/SAE 21434 Status
SR-1 Supply Chain Risk Management Policy Vendor Management Policy documented and reviewed annually; defines risk tiers (Critical / High / Medium / Low), due diligence requirements per tier, and ongoing monitoring cadence. CC5.3 5.1 — SR.2.150 7.1 ✅ Implemented
SR-2 Supply Chain Risk Management Plan Vendor register maintained with risk tier classification, due diligence artifacts, contract dates, and reassessment schedule; quarterly review of Critical vendor posture. CC9.2 5.21 — SR.2.150 7.1 ✅ Implemented
SR-3 Supply Chain Controls & Processes Security addendum required in all vendor contracts; right-to-audit clause included for Critical/High vendors; minimum security requirements defined and contractually enforced. CC9.2 5.19, 5.21 — SR.3.169 7.3 ✅ Implemented
SR-6 Supplier Assessments & Reviews Critical and High vendors reassessed annually: SOC 2 Type II report review, security questionnaire, and breach monitoring. Medium vendors every 2 years. Results recorded in vendor register. CC9.2 5.22 — SR.2.150 7.4 ✅ Implemented
SR-8 Notification Agreements 72-hour breach notification clause in all DPAs with vendors processing customer data; 30-day advance notice required before adding new subprocessors; subprocessor list updated and customers notified. CC9.2 5.20 — SR.2.150 — ✅ Implemented
SA-9 External System Services All external system connections assessed before onboarding; SOC 2 Type II (or ISO 27001) required for Critical/High vendors; DPA required for any vendor processing personal or customer data. CC9.2 5.19, 5.22 — SR.2.150 7.1, 7.4 ✅ Implemented
PS-7 External Personnel Security Contractors and consultants screened to the same standard as employees before receiving access; access time-bounded (max 90 days, renewable); scope restricted to stated engagement; access revoked on Day 0 of contract end. CC9.2 5.19, 5.20 — SR.2.150 — ✅ Implemented
AC-20 Use of External Systems External system connections inventoried; data flow to external systems documented in data inventory; prohibited categories (unapproved AI tools, personal cloud storage) defined in Acceptable Use Policy. CC9.2 5.19, 5.23 — SR.2.150 — ✅ Implemented
