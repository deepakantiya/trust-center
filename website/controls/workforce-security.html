Workforce Security Controls

NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5 — AT (Awareness & Training), PS (Personnel Security), PL (Planning) families

7Controls
4Frameworks
7/7Implemented
SOC 2 ISO 27001 CMMC L2 ISO/SAE 21434

Workforce controls ensure that employees and contractors are screened, trained, and held accountable for their security responsibilities throughout the employment lifecycle. Evidence is collected in evidence/training/ and evidence/access-provisioning/.

Control ID Control Name Implementation SOC 2 ISO 27001 CMMC L1 CMMC L2 ISO/SAE 21434 Status
AT-2 Literacy Training & Awareness Annual security awareness training required for all workforce members; quarterly phishing simulations; completion tracked with ≥95% completion rate required before audit period end. CC1.4 6.3 — AT.2.056 5.4.2 ✅ Implemented
AT-3 Role-Based Training OWASP Top 10 and secure code review for engineers; GRC and risk management training for compliance staff; IR tabletop exercises for incident responders; automotive cybersecurity for 21434-scoped teams. CC1.4 6.3 — AT.3.058 5.4.2 ✅ Implemented
PS-3 Personnel Screening Background checks (identity, criminal, employment history) completed before access provisioned; same screening standard applied to contractors and consultants. CC1.4 6.1 — PS.2.127 5.4.2 ✅ Implemented
PS-4 Personnel Termination All logical access revoked within 24 hours of termination (immediate for involuntary); MDM remote wipe triggered; company assets returned; exit interview conducted; deprovisioning checklist signed by IT and HR. CC6.3 5.11, 6.5 AC.1.001 AC.1.001, PS.2.127 — ✅ Implemented
PS-6 Access Agreements Confidentiality and NDA signed at hire; security responsibilities acknowledged annually; contractors sign access agreement before receiving credentials; agreements retained for the duration of employment plus 3 years. CC1.4 6.2, 6.6 — PS.2.127 — ✅ Implemented
PS-8 Personnel Sanctions Documented disciplinary policy covering policy violations, data misuse, and malicious activity; sanctions escalate from warning to termination and law enforcement referral; all actions documented and reported to CISO. CC1.5 6.4 — PS.2.127 — ✅ Implemented
PL-4 Rules of Behavior Acceptable Use Policy defines permitted and prohibited activities for all Company assets; remote work and clear desk requirements included; acknowledged at hire and at each annual review; AI and GenAI use standard included. CC5.3 5.10, 6.2 — AT.2.056 — ✅ Implemented
